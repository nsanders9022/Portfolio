About Me

I am a junior web developer, recently finishing a six-month bootcamp in Seattle, WA called Epicodus. Through the use of pair programming and the flipped classroom design, I learned the fundamentals of web development. I love to travel and this love stemmed from my experience growing up in Switzerland and England. My understanding of different cultures and ideas make me very open to the needs of different users, improving the designs I create.
